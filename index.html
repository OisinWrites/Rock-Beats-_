How it works

Each choice of either, rock, paper, scissors, lizard, spock, has both two options it will beat, and two that defeat it. Each choice will only tie against itself. When you select an option from the buttons above, the computer will pick a random option also. The game will determine a winner, and list your previous moves as you progress through a game of bests of five. Best of luck!
